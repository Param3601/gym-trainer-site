Frequently Asked Questions

Select your state and city on the homepage, browse available trainers, view their profile, and click "Book This Trainer". Fill in your details, select duration and time slot, then proceed to payment.

We accept all major credit and debit cards. For testing, use card number 4242 4242 4242 4242 with any future expiry date and CVV.

Please contact your trainer directly using the contact details provided after payment. Cancellation policies vary by trainer, so discuss any changes directly with them.

Trainer contact details (phone and email) are revealed after successful payment. You can find them on the confirmation page and also view them from your trainer's profile if you have a paid booking.

We recommend reading trainer reviews before booking. If you're not satisfied, you can leave a review to help other users. For major issues, contact our support team.

View available coupons in the Coupons section of your profile. Copy the code and apply it during checkout. Discount will be automatically applied to your total.
